Kill the bugs

Instructions

  • Use mouse to control the direction of your hero
  • You can press esc to pause and resume
  • Press space key to fire the bullet (Long press and release create bigger powerful bullet)
  • There will be a power up can upgrade the bullet after you score 5 points
    (Dont miss it somehow it only makes your bullet "looks" powerful)
  • First boss will show up after you kill 20 bugs
  • Second boss will come after you kill 40 bugs
  • If boss timer count down 30 seconds, there will be a weapon upgrade,
    have it and your weapon will automatically trace the boss for ten seconds
  • Three scenario you will lose one live:
    The little bug reach the left side of canvas
    Hero touch the little bug
    H it by boss bullet
  • Cheat code available below: (Hint: favorite class in Langara)

Enter both lives and bugs amount and click start button in the game area

Once upon a time, there was a Langara student working on his final
project for CPSC1045. Somehow he found there were a lot of bugs in his
code. Can you help him to kill those bugs and save his GPA?
